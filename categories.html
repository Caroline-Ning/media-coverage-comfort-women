Categories

Category Proportions, Top 4 Categories, Trendings, Central State Media vs. Local Media

01

As demonstrated, the top categories of the articles are Japan-South Korea Disagreement (23.68%), Criticize improper view in Japan (16.4%), Personal Story, Memorials, Academic Studies, Exhibition (9.79%), and Twenty-two (8.27%). Twenty-Two is a documentary on the current life of China's comfort women survivors. Because of its large article counts in the corpus, Twenty-two is listed as an independent category to inspect its degree of significance. Technically, it can be included in another category, Literary works (3.99%). Therefore, the third largest article group is Twenty-two + Literary works (8.27%+3.99%=12.26%). The top 4 categories of articles account for 62.13% of the corpus. In these categories, the word "comfort women" is located in two contexts: politics and humanities.

02

The above graph demonstrates the article count of the top 4 categories and their proportion in each year's total articles. The top 2 categories, "Criticize improper view in Japan" and "Japan-South Korea disagreement," both emphasize comfort women's political significance and construct the narrative from the national discourse. Movies such as Twenty-two and articles in the "Personal Story, Memorials, Academic Studies, Exhibition" locate comfort women in a humanities context in recent years.

03

This graph shows the ranking of the top categories changed over the years. Eleven places are selected because this makes the graph more comprehensive for the top categories: each year's 11th category has a higher ranking in another year. It demonstrates the calming down of "Twenty-two," the resilience of "Japan-South Korea Disagreement," and the reappearance of "Criticize Improper View in Japan" over the years.

04

This graph is sorted based on the difference between the groups, so the upper categories in the bar chart have a more significant difference. In general, the difference is slight because they face the same censorship in China, and they may both receive funding from the government. However, the central state media is more directly influenced by political factors, while the local media cover more domestic news that has the potential to attract the public. The reasons for these features are that, firstly, local media have very few foreign correspondents to report on foreign news, so they may focus more on domestic news; secondly, narrative or commentary domestic news may be more convenient to attract audience compared to serious political news.
